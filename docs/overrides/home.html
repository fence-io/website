What do we do ? ¶

  • {% include ".icons/fontawesome/solid/scroll.svg" %}

    Test and review policy engines

    Navigating through all available technologies is not easy. Picking up the best policy engine for a given use case can take quite some research. We deeply test and review various policy engines, helping making the decision.

  • {% include ".icons/fontawesome/solid/paw.svg" %}

    Compare and publish analysis

    Tested and reviewed technologies and products are aggregated together. We provide comparison charts across different aspects to help determining which tools do and how.

  • {% include ".icons/fontawesome/solid/blog.svg" %}

    Record podcasts regularly

    As a community we want to help showcasing policy engines in a non opinionated way. Recording podcasts with guests regularly offers an opportunity to discuss and discover more tools and technologies.

  • {% include ".icons/fontawesome/solid/gear.svg" %}

    Provide hands-on tutorials

    Our roots live in the tech world. We absolutely need to get our hands dirty. Sharing our experiments in the form of hands-on tutorials is part of our mission.

  • {% include ".icons/fontawesome/solid/hand.svg" %}

    Build a community
